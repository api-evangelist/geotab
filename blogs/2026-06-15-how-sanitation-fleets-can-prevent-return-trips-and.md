Return trips drain sanitation fleet budgets, and without documented evidence of why pickups were skipped, operators may be forced to dispatch replacement trucks at significant cost. The post shows how technology can help sanitation teams prove why certain pickups were skipped, settle service disputes, and avoid expensive second truck rolls.
